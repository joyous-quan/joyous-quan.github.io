About Us


SunBy Pharma, Committed to supplying pharmaceutical intermediates, located in Hyderabad, the sixth biggest city and well-known manufacturer center in India.

Relying on a great supplying chain from China and more than 1000 square meters storage center in Hyderabad, the fastest delivery service can be assured. And basing on laboratory R&D center in Shanghai, customers various demand can be satisfied.

Now company cooperates with BLD pharma, which is the biggest pharmaceutical intermediate supplier and manufacturer from China, to develop India pharmaceutical market, we do our best to meet the different needs of customers at different stages.

In future, SunBy Pharma will build large-scale products and services network channel, help customers to shorten products development cycle, reduce R&D and production cost, achieve win-win situation.
