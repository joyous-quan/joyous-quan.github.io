Quotations, Prices and Payment Terms

Quotation

All quotations are valid for 30 days without exception.

Prices

All prices are in U.S. dollars and FOB prices. Prices are subject to change without prior notice. Shipment will be made promptly even if prices have been nominally increased. Written quotations are guaranteed for 30 days.

Payment

Terms of payment are net thirty (30) days from Seller's date of invoice unless otherwise agreed in writing. Seller reserves the right to charge interest at the rate of 1.5% per month for all amounts that are more than thirty (30) days past due. Seller reserves the right to require full advance payment in certain cases, for any reason. Buyer is responsible for bank charges for all overseas transactions.
